About

Likan Zhan is an assistant professor of Cognitive Psychology at Beijing Language and Culture University. The following image is called The brain in a vat, which is the philosophical basis of one of Likan’s favorite movies The Matrix. This movie series raises two important questions a cognitive scientist wants to answer: How our mind works and how our brain enables the mind. To be specific, Likan Zhan’s interests include language processing, language acquisition, Autism, and theory of mind etc.
